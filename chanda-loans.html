Process

Get Cash in 4 Simple Steps

No paperwork mountains, no waiting weeks. Bring your item, walk out with cash — it's that simple.

1
Bring Your Item

Visit our office with the item you'd like to use as collateral. We accept electronics, phones, appliances, and vehicles.

2
Free Valuation

Our team assesses your item's market value on the spot. We offer up to 60% of the item's current value as a loan.

3
Sign & Collect Cash

Agree to the loan terms, sign a simple agreement, and receive your cash immediately — no delays.

4
Repay & Reclaim

Repay within the agreed period and collect your item. Need more time? Renewal options are available.
